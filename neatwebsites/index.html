Dax009's collection of neat websites

Some websites I found around the web that I think are useful, cool, or "neat". God I should stop saying the word "neat"
FYI: I don't own or take credit for any of these websites and am not responsible if they lead to anything illegal, inaccurate, or anything like that. I just find them helpful. If you own a website here and would like it to be removed please email me.
cobalt - save what you love without ads, trackers, or other creepy bullshit.
I visit this like, semi-daily to download stuff from websites. Works for most things you'd need and doesn't have weird ads.
PCPartPicker - Pick parts. Build your PC. Compare and share.
Very useful when building a PC, or if you're like me, just really bored. Checks compatibility with parts when making a list, compiles many retailers, and lots more.
Terms of Service; Didn't Read (ToS;DR)
Basically a repository of summaries of Terms of Service agreements for various websites. Gives you some insight into the stuff you agree to when signing up for stuff.
endoflife.date
A collection of end-of-life dates for various products, services, operating systems, software, etc.
Catbox
You can upload stuff here. Good for sharing stuff on Discord. 200MB limit.
fxtwitter.com/FixTweet
Good for embedding Twitter links into Discord. Works like 10x better than the original Twitter embed. vxTwitter exists too if that's your thing.
Snapdrop - The easiest way to transfer files across devices
Transfer files across the network. Useful for transferring between PC and phone. FYI: the site doesn't have a great track record for always being up so be aware.
Monkeytype | A minimalistic, customizable typing test
It's basically just a typing test. Title of the website says it all. Fun fact: I got 160 WPM on a school Chromebook on there and I haven't been able to beat that.
